Eggs, flour and BLOOD

Eggs, flour and BLOOD is an animated short movie that i, Teo Casoli, the awsome Maya Perotti and the incredible Chiara Helm made as our final project for the graduation in IED The story is set in a small medieval village where a little sweet girl wants to become an executioner, but sadly her family of long-tradition bakers does not approve. Luckily for her, the famous torture talent show has arrived in town, so she attends it and shows everyone that she is worth her family’s approval. At the conclusion of the short, she manages to fulfill her dream of becoming the villager’s executioner and making her family proud of her.
