Teo Casoli Animations

Hi, I’m Teo Casoli, an Italian-based animator.

I raised in Fabriano, a small town (in the middle of nowhere) famous for the paper,and i had the luck to be in costant search of new activities to do like sports, travelling and, most importantly, drawing.

I can’t live without pushing myself every day into new experiences, hoping to find new things to learn and to appreciate.

I have a very, really much, great extroversity, which helps me bond with new people and keep me forward through everything.

I like characters designing very much, and also constantly looking for new ideas and imagining the world and the stories the characters could fit in, trying not to be too ordinary into the designs.

Animation, altough, is what i really love to do: creating the concept of action and seeing it becoming alive, improving myself everyday and learning how to be more efficent during animating. My art style is inspired by moderns cartoons, and it is charactarized by a clean drawing stroke, brights colors and a lot of dinamicity!
